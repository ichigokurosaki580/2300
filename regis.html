1. Read all instructions first
Check required fields (often marked with * or bold).
Prepare necessary documents (ID, email, phone number, etc.).
2. Fill in personal details clearly
Typical fields:
Full Name: Use complete legal name.
Address: Write current address, including barangay/city.
Birthdate: Use the required format (e.g., MM/DD/YYYY).
Contact Number: Use an active number.
Email: Use a working email you can access.
3. Provide additional required info
Examples:
Emergency contact
Parent/guardian details
Membership type
Preferred schedule
4. Double-check spelling
Make sure your name, email, and numbers are correct.
5. Review before submitting
Confirm all needed fields are completed. Submit only once unless told otherwise.
