1. Answer honestly
Surveys rely on real feedback.
2. Follow the question type
Common types:
Multiple choice → Select only one unless “Check all that apply.”
Rating scale → Rate properly (1–5, 1–10).
Yes/No → Choose the correct one.
Short answer → Keep answers clear and direct.
Long answer → Provide details, but stay on-topic.
3. Do not skip mandatory questions
They’re often marked with an asterisk (*).
4. Stay neutral if unsure
Use options like:
“Not applicable”
“Prefer not to answer”
“I don’t know” 5. Review before submitting Make sure your answers make sense
